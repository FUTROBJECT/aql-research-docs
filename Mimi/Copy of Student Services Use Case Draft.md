employment* **Resume and interview prep:** AI-assisted resume builders, mock interviews, cover letter generators, etc. **Skills-to-job matching:** Matches student profiles to local job postings based surfaces remaining gaps **Enrollment nudge campaigns:** Automatically reaches out to students with reminders and flags | *Simplifying and improving access to financial support* **FAFSA completion assistant:** guidance with deadline nudges and plain-language answers **Scholarship matching:** Matches student profiles to scholarships, auto-populates applications **Financial dashboard:** Real-time balance, aid status, payment dates, and next steps | *Limiting barriers for students* **Accommodation request portal:** Guided intake matching documentation to accommodations, generates faculty notifications **Assistive tech matching:** Recommends tools based on student needs and existing campus licenses        **Compliance tracking:** Automates accommodation letters, flags non-compliance | *Fostering belonging for all students*  **Campus life recommender:** Suggests clubs, events, activities based on program, interests, schedule, etc. **Orientation assistant:** 24/7 onboarding for common questions (e.g., parking, ID cards, course access) **Basic needs screener:** Identifies food, housing, or transportation insecurity, routes to resources | *Streamlining compliance and reporting*  **Guided reporting intake:** Walks students through reporting options (e.g., Title IX, conduct) with clear next steps **Policy explainer:** Answers rights and process questions, drawing
